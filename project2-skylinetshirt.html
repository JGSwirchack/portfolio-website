Skyline Vapor T-Shirt Design

Create a T-Shirt design for their physical store front for Vape supplies, e-Liquid, and lounge. The idea was to create merchandise for the store “Skyline Vapor”, a simple graphic with the store name and a visual. Being the Graphic Design Manager, it was my task to review employee suggestions, but ultimately decided on my own design. The idea was to illustrate the concept of a healthier alternative to smoking incorporating e-Liquid for vaping. For this I incorporated a vape pen with the Caduceus symbol and splatters of e-liquid.
