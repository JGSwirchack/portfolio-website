My work

Versus Our Master Logo

This project was to create the logo for my Extreme Gothic Metal band, “Versus Our Master”. The idea was to incorporate the initials of the band name into a stylized graphic logo. Being a Designer and a founding member of the band, I took on the sole responsibility for design from concept to completion. The creative took many forms but the final design was to be very simplified in black and white so that could be colorized when necessary and used across many platforms.

Skyline Vapor T-Shirt Design

Create a T-Shirt design for their physical store front for Vape supplies, e-Liquid, and lounge. The idea was to create merchandise for the store “Skyline Vapor”, a simple graphic with the store name and a visual. Being the Graphic Design Manager, it was my task to review employee suggestions, but ultimately decided on my own design. The idea was to illustrate the concept of a healthier alternative to smoking incorporating e-Liquid for vaping. For this I incorporated a vape pen with the Caduceus symbol and splatters of e-liquid.

Eric P. Mandat Black Swirls CD package

A Clarinetist/Composer wanted to have a simple CD Package design, with the focus being on the cover illustration. His idea was was to use an image of a clarinet on black background distorted into a swirl pattern to illustrate the title of the album, “Black Swirls”. I obtained a photo, did the image manipulation, and typographic layout for not only the cover, but the entire CD package as well.
