Eric P. Mandat: Black Swirls CD Package Design

This project was to create the logo for my Extreme Gothic Metal band, “Versus Our Master”. The idea was to incorporate the initials of the band name into a stylized graphic logo. Being a Designer and a founding member of the band, I took on the sole responsibility for design from concept to completion. The creative took many forms but the final design was to be very simplified in black and white so that could be colorized when necessary and used across many platforms.
